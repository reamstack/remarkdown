Words ending in -chen or -lein are neutral

das Brötchen 

the bread roll

das Mädchen

the girl

das Fräulein

miss (old-fashioned)

words ending in -um

das Datum

the date

das Museum

the museum

words ending in -ment

das Document

the document

das Sortiment

the assortment

words ending in -nis

das Ergebnis

the result

das Verhältnis

the relation, the proportion

Names of cafes, hotels and restaurants are also neutral.

das Hilton

das Ritz

Most nouns starting with Ge- are neutral

das Gemüse

the vegetables

das Gebirge

the mountains

das Geschirr

the dishes

Most of the 112 chemical elements are neutral

das Aluminium

the aluminum

das Gold

the gold

das Zink

the zinc

Infinitives used as nouns (gerunds) are neutral

das Treffen

the meeting

das Rauchen

smoking

das Schreiben

writing

Colors are neutral.

das Blau

blue

das Rot

red

das Gelb

yellow

das Grün

green
